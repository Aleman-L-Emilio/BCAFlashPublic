About

What:                                            

​BCA Flash is a one-day event where Bergen County Academies students teach courses regarding their own academies (science and technology, medical, engineering, business, culinary, computer science, visual arts, music, & theater) to middle school students (6th-8th grade) who reside in Bergen County.
​2022 NOTE: Unfortunately, it has been decided that instead of a physical or virtual event for BCA Flash 2022, we will be providing ONLINE VIDEO RESOURCES to middle school students! You may find these videos uploaded to this website under the "Videos" page.
