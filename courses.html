Courses 

BCA Flash 2019 Courses

Please look for your top 4 courses!

​*Only has 2 sessions rather than the usual 4 sessions


Make Your Own Compass: Science of Magnetism

Students will learn about the concept of magnetism. This course will provide students with a great knowledge of eddy currents through an experiment. Students will get to make their own compasses, and even take them home!

Periodic Table

In this course, students will learn about the history of the periodic table. Students will look more closely at the periodic table to understand the organization of elements to identify trends. They can define what elements, atoms, and molecules are, and what the periodic table reveals about their properties. Topics include early atomic theory, atomic number, and atomic mass for each element, and periodic trends.

Linear Momentum

Momentum is the quantifier of objects in motion. Its applications and real-life consequences will be discussed. Students will learn about conservation of momentum, impulse, and collisions!

 Intro to Chemistry - Molar Conversions

This course will teach students the basics of chemistry, and will be focused mainly on the Mole Highway (not the animal/the black mark!) and using it to convert between various units. This concept is an essential part of chemistry as it is the basis of various calculations. A fun, hands-on experiment will follow in which students will use their newly learned skills and apply them in the lab under the supervision of staff members.

 CSI Forensics Lab FULL

In this course, students will get to experiment like forensic scientists by identifying unknown white powders. To do this, students will test how known powders react to various liquids and compare results with the unknown sample. Candy prizes will be available to students through active participation and correct identification.

Balancing the Building Blocks of Chemistry

The objective of this course is to develop a solid foundation of basic principles of chemistry and chemical equations.

The Galaxy and Beyond: Introduction to Astrophysics FULL

This course is an introduction to the fascinating world of astrophysics! We will introduce a variety of interesting topics in the field, such as black holes, types of stars, and the expansion of the universe. We will also talk about some of the newer theories about parallel universes and higher dimensions, and discuss gravitational waves and the relevance of that to modern day work in astrophysics. We will also have discussions about which theories you think are accurate and like the best, as well as activities to aid in the lesson.

Marketing 101 

This class will serve as an introductory course for marketing. Marketing is a set of process for creating, communicating, and delivering value to customers and for managing customer relations to benefit the organization stakeholders. The course will focus on the components of the marketing mix, including product, price, promotion, and place. Students will also be able to identify forces that constitute the external marketing environment and different market segmentations. Activities using existing products on the market will be utilized to reinforce the comprehension of concepts.

Keeping Up With the Clock

Do you have a hard time keeping up with work? Is the clock certainly not your friend? To help deal with these problems that middle school students begin to develop, we have formed a course on stress and time management. This course will help students to better understand the concept of time and how they can find ways to organize their schedule in a feasible and beneficial way. With this, we hope to teach students how to set limits and goals for themselves in terms of schoolwork, allowing for a decrease in stress levels. In addition, we look forward to asking every student questions about what they consider is the best way to deal with such aspects of life, in order to show that stress and time management is different for everyone. Our ultimate goal is to give the students something to take home with them, which we hope is a new perspective on time, and knowledge on the stress-relief process.

Intro to Banking FULL

Banks are critical to our economy. As a key component of the financial system, banks allocate funds from savers to borrowers in an efficient manner. They provide specialized financial services, which reduce the cost of obtaining information about both savings and borrowing opportunities. In this course, students will learn about the how a bank works, the different types of banks, how banks make money, and the types of services they provide.

 *Intro to Culinary* FULL

For the Intro to Culinary course, students will first review a presentation on kitchen safety and safe food handling. While in the kitchen, instructors will supervise students as they make double chocolate cookies and pesto pinwheels. Upon completing the class, students will have learned how to operate various pieces of kitchen equipment, such as mixers and bench scrapers, in addition to basic culinary techniques, such as emulsion.

*Intro to 3D Printing* FULL

Students will learn the process of 3D printing by learning the basics of CAD design using AutoDesk Inventor, and how to export the files they create to be used for 3D printing. Students will also get to see 3D printers in use, and take home a 3D printed trinket.

*Everyday Engineering*  

Students will learn about simple machines and and how they are present in our daily lives. Throughout the course we will discuss the basics of engineering, ways to logically calculate their mechanical advantage, and finally create a fun hands on activity that completes our demonstration of how engineering is used by everyone everyday.

*Mousetrap Racers*

On your mark, get set, go! By harnessing the power of simple mousetraps, you will learn all about the principles of engineering and mechanical advantage as well as apply those concepts into something that is fun and competitive.

*The Science Behind Catapults*

Ever wanted to learn how catapults work? Ever wanted to build a catapult? If you answered "yes" to either of these questions, then this is the right course for you! By joining this course, you will learn the science behind catapults, as well as be provided with materials in order to use the engineering design process to make one on your own!

*A Fun Time with Projectile Motion!*

Students will learn the exciting physics behind throwing rocks and launching marbles! In this course, students will be introduced to the basics of vectors, motion in one dimension, and motion in two dimensions. The students in this course will gain a hands on lab experience that will allow them to understand how projectile motion works and apply it to the real world in a fun experiment! The students, after learning the appropriate physics, will compete to see who can launch a marble most accurately. The winner will gain bragging rights, invaluable knowledge, and a prize specially 3D printed from our own facilities.

Molecular and Cognitive Neuroscience

Molecular, or cellular, neuroscience focuses on the mechanisms by which information flows within and among cells in the nervous system and the mechanisms through which the cellular structure of the nervous system develops and is maintained. Cognitive neuroscience focuses on how cognitive functions, such as vision or language, are implemented by the brain. In this course, students will learn the basics of both areas of neuroscience.

Introduction to the Medical Field

Have you ever wanted to experience what it would be like to be part of the medical field? There is so much to be and do and taking this class will help give a deep insight into it. We will go over different professions (i.e physicians, researchers, nurses, and EMTs). To make it more fun, we are going to have exciting scenarios in which you are playing the various roles in the medical field. The course goes deeper into the understanding of what it takes to be part of the medical field and how interesting it can get.

It's All in Your Genes?

Welcome to “It’s All in Your Genes?”! Does anyone ever tell you that you have “good genes” and you have no idea what they’re talking about? If so, then you’re in luck! In this course, you will be able to learn all about basic genetics. The course will first provide you with insight on what the field of genetics is. Next, it will discuss the effect that genes have on the human body. Leading into a fun activity, the course will dive into the microscopic scale of genetics—DNA structure and function! You will learn all about the four simple nucleotides that amazingly dictate the way everyone you know looks, talks, acts, and thinks! At the end of the class, prepare yourself for a hands-on experience in which you will get to create your own edible DNA molecule that you can take home! From what genetics do for us in the real world to what they do on the microscopic scale, throughout this course, you will be able gain a solid understanding of what it is that makes you, you!

Disease and Diagnosis

This course will take a look at several rare diseases and the way that they work biologically. Students will be able to learn about these diseases and then do case studies, where they will be challenged to identify the diseases that they learned about based on symptoms. It will be a good way to encourage raising awareness about these rare conditions and to let more people know about them and make a difference in the world.

*Dev the Web!*

Have you ever wanted to build your own website? Did you ever wonder how some of your favorite websites work? In this course, you will look into a special language called HTML. By the end of class, you’ll become familiar with the fundamentals of this language to be able to start building a website of your own!

*Python Fun* FULL

Ever want to experience the world of programming, but feel discouraged just looking at a beginners guide? This course offers an intuitive and easy run down of the basics of programming in python!

Got Rhythm?

Students will learn the basics of rhythm and music notation through fun activities and games. They will be able to try out different instruments in order to create their own, original compositions, culminating in a performance at the end of the class.a

Improv 101

Improv 101 gives students the opportunity to explore their inner thespian and have fun while doing so. Through various activities and improv games, students will work on both public speaking and decision making skills.These skills are essential for any individual hoping to boost their confidence and creativity. Improv allows students to work on these important life skills in a fun and supportive atmosphere.

*p5.js: Creative Programming* FULL

P5.js is a javascript library that enables artists and designers to create graphics and animations utilizing many of the foundational elements of javascript, a coding language often associated with web design and other useful platforms. As javascript is widely used in many fields alongside popular coding languages such as HTML and CSS, it is ambiguous in usage and can be a valuable asset in any field. This class will be introducing important concepts of javascript and coding languages in general, while still offering a fun and creative payoff in the form of creating artistic masterpieces with the p5.js editor.

*Foundation of Visual Arts*

This course will be centered around foundation techniques of art. Students will learn how to use the basic elements of line and color through exercises and exploration. Students will also be encouraged to use their creativity to add to the foundational skills that are taught.
