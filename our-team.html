From Norwood, New Jersey, Justin Oh was in the Academy for the Advancement of Science and Technology (AAST). As the Founder of BCA Flash, Justin created this event so that middle school students can get a hands-on experience in the field of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics). He also wanted to show the diversity in the knowledge Bergen County Academies students possess. Moreover, Justin was interested in both the medical and business fields, and pursued his senior internship at Eisai Inc. in the Emerging Markets department. Apart from BCA Flash, Justin was a part of BCA's Model UN team, a food social media influencer, and an avid Ovarian Cancer researcher in the BCA Nano-Structural Imaging Lab. He looks forward to seeing how BCA Flash will live on in 2022!
