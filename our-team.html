From Norwood, New Jersey, Justin Oh is a senior in the Academy for the Advancement of Science and Technology (AAST).  As the Founder and Organizer of BCA Flash, Justin created this event so that middle school students can get a hands-on experience in the field of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics). He also wanted to show the diversity in the knowledge Bergen County Academies students possess. Moreover, Justin is interested in both medical and business fields, and is currently pursuing his senior internship at Eisai Inc. in the Emerging Markets department. Apart from BCA Flash, Justin is a part of BCA's Model UN team, a food social media influencer, and an avid Ovarian Cancer researcher in the BCA Nano-Structural Imaging Lab. He looks forward to meeting everyone who will come to BCA Flash 2020!
